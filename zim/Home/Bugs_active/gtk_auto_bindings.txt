====== gtk auto bindings ======
Created Wednesday 14 December 2016

Use GObjectIntrospection to generate SWT bindings.
https://wiki.gnome.org/Projects/GObjectIntrospection 

SWT Cocoa does this:
bundles/org.eclipse.swt/Eclipse SWT PI/cocoa/org/eclipse/swt/internal/cocoa/AppKitFull.bridgesupport 

gir files are XML files that provide binding info:
[[/usr/share/gir-1.0/Gtk-3.0.gir]]
