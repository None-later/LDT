====== rsync ======
Created Tuesday 30 August 2016

===== Syncing two local directories: =====
Good introduction to rsync:https://www.digitalocean.com/community/tutorials/how-to-use-rsync-to-sync-local-and-remote-directories-on-a-vps

	**rsync -r --delete source/ destination**
Observe:
- '/' after source. Means copy content of dir instead of dir itself into destination dir.
- destination is created if not exists
- '-r' means recursive
- '--delete' remove clones 

===== Examples =====
Good examples:
http://www.tecmint.com/rsync-local-remote-file-synchronization-commands/

ex:
rsync -avzh /home/lufimtse/workspace_neon/ [[/home/lufimtse/workspace]]

===== With progress indicator: =====
rsync -azh --info=progress2 /home/lufimtse/workspace_neon/ ~/workspace
rsync -azh --info=progress2 /home/lufimtse/apps/eclipse ~/
