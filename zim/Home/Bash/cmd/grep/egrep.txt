====== egrep ======
Created Thursday 20 April 2017

regex oriented grep. Doesn't require escaping of characters.

ex:
egrep "^gtk(2|3)
vs
grep "^gtk\(3\|3\)"



src:
http://www.cs.columbia.edu/~tal/3261/fall07/handout/egrep_mini-tutorial.htm
