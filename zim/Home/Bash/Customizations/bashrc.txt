===== Colouring of PS1 =====
# Color my PS1:
PS1='\[\e[1;34m\][\t \w]$ \[\e[m\]'
# https://www.linux.com/learn/how-make-fancy-and-useful-bash-prompt-linux
# e1:34 -  1=bold, 34=color.  
# \[\e[m\]  - stop color
# \t - time 
# \w - working dir. (full)
# \W - working dir (basename)
