====== substring and subset ======
Created Monday 27 June 2016

${PARAMETER:OFFSET:LENGTH}
paramater == variable, can be “@” for pos-args like ${@:2:3}
length is optional
e.g

$ x="Leo Ufimtsev"
$ echo ${x:1:2}  
eo
