==== recordmydesktop - Screen recording ====
**Package:**
	gtk-recordmydesktop
	// qt-rec... and others exist. You want the gtk- one.
**Usage:**
	1) *first* click "save as"  (not after). If not done, output saved as ~/out.ogv
	2) Select area to record etc..
	3) Stop recording by pressing square in bottom tray.

See also: https://fedoraproject.org/wiki/ScreenCasting
