====== nice ======
Created Tuesday 15 May 2018

* -20 (highest) to 19 (lowest, nicest)
* Only root can lower nice value.

Ex:
nice -10 LargJob   # Sets nice value to  10
nice --10 LargeJob # Sets nice value to -10
renice 17 -p 1134
